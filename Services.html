The process always begins with evaluating an author’s goals, and their work. Each link below will direct you to a detailed explanation of what to expect from that phase of our services stream. If it sounds right for you, simply fill out the intake form as prompted. Don’t forget to attach any files or links if asked to do so, then submit your response. You should receive a reply by email within 5 business days, which will explain our evaluation of your work, and provide further instructions.

FAQ’s

Does Wildwynd Publishing take control of my copyrights? Only if the author chooses to sell us those rights via the traditional publishing stream. Self publishers retain the copyrights to their work, granting us a contracted licence to produce and distribute that work in exchange for royalties and/or fees

Do I need to submit a finished piece of work? Not necessarily! You may submit draft material for editing and design evaluations as well, provided that it meets our submission requirements.

If my submission is rejected, can I try again? If a submission is rejected we will always provide an explanation, and in some cases, directions for improvement and re-submission. In other cases, this will not be considered an option.

Can Wildwynd Publishing sell or promote my existing book? We can sell books published by other companies, and we can promote them, provided that this is not forbidden by any contract between the author and their current publisher. Consultation and strategy development services are available to everyone.
