We're always happy to hear it!

We think of our authors as a part of the team, and we treat them as such. Each of the titles published by our house was the direct result of adapting to each author's unique needs, and working with them to find a solution. Their solution. In our minds, there is no other way to do it right. Staying in touch is a vital part of the process.

Customers should expect regular email updates from the project manager assigned to their case, and project related inquiries should be directed to this person.
