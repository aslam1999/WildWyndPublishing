WELCOME!

At Wildwynd Publishing,
we turn writers into authors,
and readers into fans.

Our company began as a small traditional publisher, but we have since expanded to include a complete set of services for self publishers as well. Because one way or another, our goal is to help authors get their books into the hands of readers.

Our portfolio covers a broad range of fiction and non-fiction material. We take a special interest in stories and subjects which are in some way unconventional, because we believe that in diversity there is strength. That is why we work hard to make sure every author gets a truly authentic voice. Our four step support process includes:

  • Manuscript evaluation and editing
  • Cover and page design
  • Print and Ebook distribution
  • Marketing strategies and materials
